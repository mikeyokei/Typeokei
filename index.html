Typographic Terms

Font vs Typeface

Font refers to a specific style and size, while typeface is a family of related fonts.

Broad Categories

Serif, Sans Serif, Script

Kerning, Spacing, Leading

Kerning: space between letters. Spacing: overall distance between letters and words. Leading: distance between lines.
